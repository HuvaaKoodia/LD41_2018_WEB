That Which Cannot Be Contained


Dodge bullets and destroy enemies in this top-down-dodge-em-up-bullet-hell-match-3 combo.


Instructions


- Hold down the grab button while aiming at a block (from any distance).

- Release the grab button to throw the block (in any direction).

- Combine 2 red or 3 magenta blocks for explosive results!

- Adjacent adversaries will be terminated (1 point for each!)


Keyboard:


WASD or QZSD = movement

Arrows = aiming

Spacebar (hold/release) = grab/throw block

R = restart

M = mute music


Gamepad (360):


Left stick = movement

Right stick = aiming

Left or right bumper (hold/release) = grab/throw block


Start or Back = restart
